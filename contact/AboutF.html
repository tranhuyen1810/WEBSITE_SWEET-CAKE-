About Us

At FrozenSweet, we believe that every cake should be a work of art. Our mission is to create delicious and beautiful cakes that bring joy to people's lives. We are committed to using only the finest ingredients and the most innovative techniques to create cakes that are as delicious as they are beautiful.

Our Story

FrozenSweet was founded in 2022 by Chetan Raut, a passionate person who wanted to share his love of cakes with the world. Over the years, our business has grown from a small home-based operation to a thriving bakery with a loyal following. We are proud to have won numerous awards for our cakes, including Best Cake in Wardha for three years in a row.

Our Team

At FrozenSweet, we are a team of dedicated professionals such as Harshal Zilpe,Kaushal Dahekar, who are passionate about creating beautiful and delicious cakes. Our team includes expert bakers, skilled decorators, and friendly customer service representatives. We are committed to providing the highest level of service and quality to our customers.

Our Customers

Our customers are the heart and soul of our business. We are grateful for their trust and support, and we are committed to exceeding their expectations every time they order from us. We love hearing from our customers and seeing their smiles when they taste our cakes.

Our Promise

At FrozenSweet, we promise to always use the finest ingredients, the most innovative techniques, and the highest level of craftsmanship to create cakes that are as delicious as they are beautiful. We promise to treat our customers with respect, honesty, and kindness, and to always go the extra mile to ensure their satisfaction .

Thank you for choosing FrozenSweet We look forward to creating a beautiful and delicious cake for you soon.
